How It Works

  • Both players run separate RP-1 careers with the same mod list
  • Import your persistent.sfs or quicksave to update the dashboard
  • The app reads your reputation, funds, milestones, and in-game date
  • Both players can use any parts/engines - no faction restrictions

Scoring

  • Your score is your RP-1 reputation - the higher the better
  • Milestones are compared by in-game date - whoever achieves them first gets bragging rights
  • The milestone timeline shows who got there first with a gold star

Propaganda

  • Every milestone completed first generates a newspaper headline
  • Headlines appear in the Propaganda tab
  • A ghost track shows real-world NASA/USSR dates for comparison

Victory

  • The race ends when someone completes a Crewed Moon Landing
  • Highest reputation at that point wins
  • Or just keep playing and comparing - it's your race!
